Introduction

SecureConnect VPN encrypts internet traffic to safeguard your online activity, ensuring privacy and access to restricted content, even on public networks.

Logging into SecureConnect VPN

Step 1: Launch SecureConnect

  • Open the app on your device.

Step 2: Enter Credentials

  • Input your email and password.

Step 3: Authenticate (if MFA Enabled)

  • Enter the six-digit verification code from your authentication app.

Step 4: Click 'Login'

  • Press "Login" to access the dashboard.
Connection Modes and Settings

Default Mode

  • Automatically connects to the fastest server.

Streaming Mode

  • Optimized for streaming with minimal buffering.

Privacy Mode

  • Ensures maximum encryption and anonymity.

Manual Server Selection

  • Choose a server manually by clicking "Change Server" and then "Connect".
Troubleshooting and FAQs

Problem: Unable to Connect

  1. Check internet stability.
  2. Restart the app or adjust firewall/antivirus settings.

Problem: Slow Connection Speed

  1. Switch to another server.
  2. Enable "Auto Bandwidth Optimization" in settings.
